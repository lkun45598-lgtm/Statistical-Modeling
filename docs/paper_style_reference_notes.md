# Paper Style Reference Notes

## Reference Source

The folder `2023统计建模优秀论文合集/` contains 14 excellent-paper PDFs from the 2023 statistical modeling contest. Most files are scanned or image-style PDFs, so the review used rendered page thumbnails rather than text extraction.

## Observed Excellent-Paper Traits

- The first page usually begins directly with the paper title, author line, abstract, keywords, and `一、引言`.
- The body uses Chinese hierarchy: `一、` for major sections, `（一）` for subsections, `1.` for lower-level points, and occasional circled-number lists.
- The writing style is compact and result-oriented. Section openings usually state the research problem first, then the method or evidence, and then the empirical conclusion.
- The papers emphasize an argument chain: realistic problem -> indicator/model construction -> empirical measurement -> robustness or mechanism analysis -> policy implication.
- Figures and tables are embedded close to the argument. Research-framework diagrams and indicator-system tables often appear early.
- Page length is usually about 14--26 A4 pages in the excellent-paper collection.

## Official 2026 Constraints

The 2026 contest attachment requires a different submission structure:

- Word full version must contain eight parts: cover, abstract and keywords, table of contents, list of tables and figures, body, references, appendix, acknowledgement.
- Anonymous PDF removes only the cover and acknowledgement, while the remaining six parts must match the Word full version.
- School, team-member, teacher, and work-number information must not appear outside the full-version cover and acknowledgement.
- Body hierarchy should use `一、` / `（一）` / `1.` / `①`.

## Rewrite Decision

This project should keep the official 2026 submission structure and rewrite only the body presentation style. The final paper therefore remains upload-compliant, but its body organization, heading hierarchy, and result discussion are aligned with the 2023 excellent-paper style.
