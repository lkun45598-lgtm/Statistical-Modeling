# Key references and data sources

- NOAA Coral Reef Watch 5km product suite: Daily global 5km CoralTemp SST, SST anomaly, HotSpot, DHW, Bleaching Alert Area.
- NOAA CRW methodology: HotSpot, Degree Heating Week, and Bleaching Alert Area definitions.
- SimVPv2: "Towards Simple yet Powerful Spatiotemporal Predictive Learning" (2022).
- OpenSTL: comprehensive spatiotemporal predictive learning benchmark.
- TAU: Temporal Attention Unit (CVPR 2023).
- SwinLSTM: ICCV 2023 official implementation.
- CAS-Canglong: global SST subseasonal-to-seasonal forecasting model.
